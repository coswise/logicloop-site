Privacy Policy

2357: Prime Number Puzzle · LogicLoop · Last updated June 2026

What we collect

Anonymous identifier

When you first open the app, a random anonymous ID is created using Firebase Anonymous Authentication. This ID is not linked to your name, email, or any personal information. It is used solely to associate your scores with your account on the leaderboard.

Gameplay analytics

We collect anonymised events about how the app is used, including:

  • Which game modes you play
  • Game session scores, duration, and difficulty reached
  • Settings you change (sound, haptics, guided mode)
  • Screen navigation (which screens you visit)

This data is collected via Firebase Analytics and is used only to understand how people use the app so we can improve it.

Leaderboard data

If you set a username inside the app, that username and your weekly score are stored on our Firebase servers to display the group leaderboard. You choose your own username — do not use your real name if you prefer to stay anonymous.

What we do NOT collect

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your location
  • Your contacts or photos
  • Payment information
  • Any data from other apps

Third-party services

All Firebase services are operated by Google LLC under their privacy terms.

Data retention

  • Leaderboard scores are reset weekly.
  • Analytics data is retained for 14 months (Google default).
  • Anonymous authentication tokens persist until you uninstall the app.

Your rights

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app. Because we only store an anonymous ID — not your identity — we cannot fulfil data subject requests by name. Uninstalling removes your local device data, and your anonymous leaderboard entry will expire within one weekly reset cycle.

Children

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app contains no chat, social features, or advertising.
